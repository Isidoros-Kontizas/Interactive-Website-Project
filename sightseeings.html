Frequently Asked Questions

+ How do I get tickets for the Tower of London?

You can purchase tickets online or at the entrance. It's recommended to buy tickets in advance to avoid long lines.

+ What are the opening hours for the London Eye?

The London Eye is open daily from 10:00 AM to 8:30 PM, but opening hours may vary during holidays.

+ Can I take photos at Buckingham Palace?

Yes, photography is allowed at Buckingham Palace's exterior. However, photography is not allowed inside the palace.

+ Is there a way to skip the lines at the British Museum?

You can buy skip-the-line tickets or join a guided tour to avoid long wait times at the British Museum.
